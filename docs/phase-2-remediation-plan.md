# Phase 2 Remediation Plan

1. Disable or remove terminated worker accounts.
2. Assign owners to service accounts.
3. Rotate stale credentials.
4. Review permanent privileged roles.
5. Define role-to-group mappings.
6. Move critical apps to group assignment.
7. Require MFA for privileged users and high-value apps.
8. Export evidence ledger and handoff notes.
